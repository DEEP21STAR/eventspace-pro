⚡ HOW TO USE EVENTSPACE PRO
1
PROPERTY & CLIENT
Enter client details & find the property
Fill in the client's name, phone, and email. Then type the event address and click Find Property — the satellite map will zoom to the exact location.
  • Use the full street address for best map accuracy
  • Event date defaults to the next Saturday — tap to change
  • Guest count drives marquee recommendations in Step 3
2
ZONE MAPPING
Draw the available outdoor space
Click Draw Zone then tap points around the usable area on the satellite map. Double-click to finish. The app calculates the exact square metres automatically.
  • Exclude the house, driveway, and trees from your zone
  • Draw multiple zones if the space is split
  • Area updates instantly — aim for the marquee footprint + 2m clearance
3
MARQUEE & FURNITURE
Choose your marquee & package
Select a Quick Package (Silver / Gold / Platinum) or build custom. Add individual furniture items using the + buttons. Smart suggestions appear based on guest count.
  • Gold package covers up to 60 guests — most popular choice
  • Adjust seasonal pricing: Standard, Winter +10%, Peak +15%
  • Furniture quantities save automatically for the session
4
QUOTE & EXPORT
Review, adjust & send the quote
Your professional quote is generated instantly. Click any field to edit inline. Send via WhatsApp, download as PDF, or email directly. Quote is valid for 30 days.
  • Use the Manual Adjustments panel for discounts or extras
  • Tap Present for fullscreen client presentation mode
  • Hit Saved to store the quote and access it later
